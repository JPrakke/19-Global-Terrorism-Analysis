Useage

The metadata coord and attack-info routes can all be modified using any year from 2000-2016
the second variable allows input of the top 10 terrorist groups from 2000-2016:
  1. Taliban
  2. Islamic State of Iraq and the Levant (ISIL)
  3. Al-Shabaab
  4. Communist Party of India - Maoist (CPI-Maoist)
  5. Boko Haram
  6. New People's Army (NPA)
  7. Maoists
  8. Tehrik-i-Taliban Pakistan (TTP)
  9. Revolutionary Armed Forces of Colombia (FARC)
  10. Kurdistan Workers' Party (PKK)

Pass in any of the group names from the list above in place of the "All" place holder to get a
filtered list by that group. Leave "All" in place to get all results by year"
